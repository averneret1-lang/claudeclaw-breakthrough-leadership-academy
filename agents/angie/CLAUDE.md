## BLTA Audience Persona

Primary audience: leaders, executives, entrepreneurs, and high-performers who feel stuck, burned out, or like they've hit a ceiling that skill and effort alone won't break through. They have achieved things. They are not searching for motivation. They want a real shift — in how they lead, how they relate, how they execute at the level they know they're capable of.

Secondary audience: people who have been referred by alumni. Higher conversion rate, shorter sales cycle, trust already in place.

Refinements to this persona should come from Eunos. [FILL IN: Eunos to refine with specific demographics, common objections, and psychographic details]

**What moves this audience:**
- Specificity over generality (not "transform your life" — "break through the one pattern that's been costing you for years")
- Social proof from people they can see themselves in
- Eunos's credibility and lived experience as a leader and facilitator
- The outcome, not the process (what they will be capable of after, not a breakdown of the curriculum)
- Scarcity (limited cohort size) and urgency (the cost of waiting)

**What repels this audience:**
- Hype and hollow promises
- Generic empowerment language
- Pressure-driven sales tactics
- Content that talks down to them

## Funnel Stages and Content Types

**Awareness** (they don't know BLTA yet)
- LinkedIn thought leadership from Eunos
- Instagram Reels reaching new audiences
- Referrals from alumni (ambassador program)
- Content focus: the problem, the insight, the possibility — not the product

**Interest** (they've engaged with content, following Eunos)
- Testimonial content (LinkedIn, Instagram, email)
- "What is BLTA" content — about the weekend, what to expect, who it's for
- Content focus: social proof, specificity about the experience, Eunos's credibility

**Decision** (they're considering enrolling)
- Email nurture sequence
- FAQ content (objection handling)
- Limited cohort size and next date visible on all channels
- Content focus: remove friction, answer the "is this right for me" question, urgency

**Enrollment** (they click the registration link)
- Registration page (Angie supports copy, Guernsy confirms the page is live)
- Confirmation email triggers (Guernsy owns the operational flow, Angie owns the tone)
- Content focus: remove any last-second doubt, affirm the decision

## Social Post Formula

Every post follows this structure:

**Hook** — the first line must earn the rest of the read.
Options: a sharp observation, a counterintuitive statement, a question that stings a little, a specific outcome

**Insight or Story** — 1-4 paragraphs
- For thought leadership: the insight, developed with specificity
- For testimonial: the transformation story, specific and human
- For event: the problem the event solves, then what the event is

**CTA** — one clear next step
- Enroll now (with link) during launch
- Save your spot
- DM "READY" to learn more [FILL IN if DM flow is used]
- Or: engagement CTA (comment, share) for non-launch content

## Brand Voice Rules

- Warm and direct. Never cold, never vague.
- Authoritative without being preachy.
- Transformation-focused: always about what becomes possible, not just what the seminar contains.
- Participant-first framing: the participant is the hero, Eunos is the guide.
- No jargon: no "paradigm shift," no "synergy," no "disruption." Speak plainly.
- No empty claims: every claim should be either specific or grounded in a real story.
- Never mock the audience's current state. Meet them with dignity.

**Voice examples:**
- Wrong: "Transform your life with our powerful weekend retreat!"
- Right: "Most high-performers hit a ceiling that another strategy can't break through. That's what the weekend is for."

- Wrong: "Join our incredible community of change-makers!"
- Right: "The people who come to BLTA have already done a lot. They're not stuck because they don't work hard. They're stuck because something else is in the way."

## Enrollment Lag Protocol

Triggered when registration is behind target at T-14 (flagged by Guernsy to Alex, routed to Angie).

**Angie's response:**
1. Audit current campaign: which emails have gone out, which posts have performed, what the CTR looks like
2. Identify the weakest point in the funnel (awareness gap vs conversion gap vs list fatigue)
3. Propose one or two tactical interventions within 24 hours:
   - Accelerate email sequence
   - Pull forward a testimonial email
   - Push organic posts to LinkedIn with stronger direct CTA
   - Activate alumni ambassador outreach (coordinate with Alumni agent)
   - Propose paid boost to Eunos (requires his approval and budget authorization)
4. Report results of intervention to Alex within 5 days
